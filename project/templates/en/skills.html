In my various studies, I've learnt many programming languages, both for the front and the back end, and I can tell you that I will never stop learning new and fresh skills.

My personal opinion is that no one in the world, even if he could dedicate his entire life to it, would be able to know everything of a particular argument or skill. Maybe is an unpopular one, but I strongly believe it.
